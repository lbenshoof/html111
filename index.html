DANCE 101

*** Ballet *** Jazz *** Hip Hop *** Tap *** Modern ***


DANCE IS ONE OF THE OLDEST PAST TIMES THAT DATES BACK CENTURIES AND HAS BEEN USED FOR CELEBRATIONS, RELIGIOUS RITUALS, CUSTOME CEREMONIES, AND RELAXATION FROM ALL OVER THE WORLD. DANCE HAS EVOLVED AND BRANCHED OFF TO SO MANY DIFFERENT GENRES THERE ARE MANY THAT HAVE FUSED CREATIING NEW ONES OVER THE PAST DECADES. ONE THING ABOUT DANCE IS THERE IS ALWAYS SOMETHING NEW TO LEARN NO MATTER HOW LONG YOU HAVE BEEN DANCING. NOT ONLY IS THERE ALWAYS SOMETHING TO LEARN YOU WILL REALIZE HOW MANY DANCE MOVES AND GENRES ARE INTERTWINED AND SOMETIMES DERIVE FROM ONE ANOTHER. KNOWING THE BACKGROUND OF THE MOST POPULAR DANCES CAN POINT YOU IN THE RIGHT DIRECTION ON WHAT YOU WANT TO EXPLORE AND SEE WHAT SPARKS YOUR INTEREST!!
